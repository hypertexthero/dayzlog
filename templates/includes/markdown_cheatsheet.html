# Heading Level One

A paragraph with *emphasized* and **bold** text and a footnote[^1] referenced in the bottom of the page. 

A [link](http://www.dayzlog.com "Link hover title.") to a webpage. You can also do a reference-style [links][markdowndocs], with the reference in the bottom of the page.

- List item
- List item
    - Nested list item indented 4 spaces
        - Nested list item indented 8 spaces

## Heading Level Two

1. Ordered List item
2. Ordered List item
    1. Nested ordered list item indented 4 spaces
    2. Note that list style is controlled by the stylesheet (your numbers will be converted)
        3. Indented by 8 spaces
3. Ordered List item

Headers

Text styles

_This will also be italic_
**This text will be bold**
__This will also be bold__

*You **can** combine them*

Lists

    - Item 2b

Ordered

1. Item 1
2. Item 2
3. Item 3

    - Item 3b
